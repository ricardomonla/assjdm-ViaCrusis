EXT. JERUSALEM - DAY

A thick haze of dust hangs over the stone-paved streets. The air is heavy with the scent of dry earth and old cedar. CROWDS press against the walls, whispering in hurried Aramaic.

JESÚS (whispering)

Father, forgive them, for they know not what they do.

The Roman guards push forward, their armor clattering against the narrow alleyway walls. A woman, MARY, collapses into the dirt.

INT. THE GOVERNOR'S PALACE - CONTINUOUS

PILATE stands by the balcony, watching the chaos from a distance. He dips his hands into a stone basin of cool water.

PONTIUS PILATE

Is truth a matter of perspective, or a matter of power?

EXT. GOLGOTHA - LATER

The sky begins to bruise, deep purples and jagged greys swallowing the sun. The first heavy drop of rain hits the parched earth.
